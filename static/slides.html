How It Works

Backend

Frontend

  • Dark-themed single-page app
  • Mode selector with side-by-side results
  • Timing badges & response-time bar chart
  • Click-to-toggle mic (ElevenLabs STT)
  • Built-in browser TTS (Web Speech API)
